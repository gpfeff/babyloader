Baby Arrives in Days

So it happened like this: I was shootin' the breeze with an expectant father over the weekend and jokingly suggested a loading bar to represent the period of gestation. Enter the due date and estimated day of conception and the progress bar will increment until the due date.


Check out the source files on Github if you're into that kind of thing.


There are also three themes: one for boys, one for girls, and one for couples who are keeping it a surprise.
